Hello, This is Faisal F Rafat

Welcome to this quiet corner of the web—no noise, just vibes. I’m curious, creative, and quietly ambitious.

I like building things—mostly with no-code, sometimes with low-code, rarely with patience. Been obsessed with how digital stuff works since forever, not just as a user but as someone who likes to poke under the hood and mess around.

What I’m Doing Now

I work as a Program Officer at the EBLICT Project under Dream71 Bangladesh Ltd., where my job description basically reads: “do everything.” From paperwork to UI/UX design to testing apps—I jump between tasks like tabs on a busy Chrome window. It’s hectic, but never boring.

On the side, I take on WordPress gigs-designing, fixing, and keeping sites alive. I also tinker with my own little projects, mostly just to stay sane and keep my brain from getting rusty.

A Bit About My Academics

I studied Civil Engineering at RUET—though nothing I do now has much to do with bending steel or pouring concrete. What I really learned there was how to survive, adapt, and keep going when things get tough. Think of it as a degree in life, disguised as engineering.

I spent a lot of time organizing events and running clubs on campus, which taught me way more about teamwork and chaos management than any textbook. As for the skills I use now? Learned them all from the world’s biggest university: Google.

Oh, and I’m currently doing a professional Master’s in Applied Statistics and Data Science from JU—adding some solid numbers and analytics to the mix.

Things I'm Good At

I shoot photos with my cellphone and camera and make them pop with Lightroom. I cut and stitch videos using Premiere Pro. When it comes to design, Figma and Illustrator are my playgrounds. I build WordPress sites, dabble in coding, and even tinker with basic electronics and networking.

Oh, and I consider myself a great prompt engineer—crafting the right words to get AI to do some magic. Plus, I’m a quick learner who’s always hungry for the next skill.

What I Do for Fun

I travel. Mostly solo, mostly on a budget, and usually with no solid plan—just a backpack and a sense of adventure. So far, I’ve wandered through India, Sri Lanka, and Thailand, with many more pins to drop on the map.

I love capturing landscapes and street moments along the way, then editing them till they feel just right. Travel keeps my eyes open and my mind clear. It’s how I hit refresh.

Free Time Shenanigans

When I’m off the clock, you’ll find me binge-watching movies and YouTube, or fiddling with electronic gadgets—mostly the mysterious “China-made wonders” from AliExpress.

I build things, break things, and sometimes manage to fix them again. Oh, and I try to share those adventures on my YouTube channel for anyone curious enough to watch the chaos unfold.
